Cura(Collected)
A collective endeavor by Eduardo Restrepo, Anjuli Rathod, Oscar Moises Diaz, Sessa Englund, and with an accompanying essay by manuel arturo abreu. Hosted at the Knockdown Center, Queens, 2017.
Born out of conversations between the three participating artists, the exhibition focuses on curation as an invested act. Exploring the type of spaces curators create, the show investigates how these spaces can be expanded to include ideals of care, empathy, and nurture. With respect to their ongoing areas of focus, this collaboration will result in a presentation of each artists individual practices in a collective light.

Participating artists:
Crack Rodriguez, Dyeemah Simmons, Carmelle Safdie, Lily Jue Sheng, Lucy Tomasino, Nancy Chavarria, Veronica Vides, Justin Cabrillos & Matthew Shalzi, Mariana Herrera Castano, Nancy Chavarria, Mauricio Esquivel, Rafia, Yeiry Guevara, Julia Pimes Mata, Karina Puente, Sayre Quevedo.
