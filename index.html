Cura(Collected)
A collective endeavor by Eduardo Restrepo, Anjuli Rathod, Oscar Moises Diaz, and with an accompanying essay by manuel arturo abreu. Hosted at the Knockdown Center, Queens, 2017.

Exploring the type of spaces curators create, the show investigates how these spaces can be expanded to include ideals of care, empathy, and nurture. With respect to their ongoing areas of focus, this collaboration will result in a presentation of each artists individual practices in a collective light.

Curatorial Selection:
Crack Rodriguez, Dyeemah Simmons, Carmelle Safdie, Lily Jue Sheng, Lucy Tomasino, Nancy Chavarria, Veronica Vides, Justin Cabrillos & Matthew Shalzi, Mariana Herrera Castano, Nancy Chavarria, Mauricio Esquivel, Rafia, Yeiry Guevara, Julia Pimes Mata, Karina Puente, Sayre Quevedo.
